## Description (4000 chars max)
Markets move. Your emotions don't have to.

baln is a daily 5-minute habit that helps you understand why your portfolio moved today -- so you never panic-sell again.

Built by a former fund manager who watched countless investors lose money not because of bad picks, but because of emotional reactions. baln gives you the context to stay calm.

--- HOW IT WORKS ---

Every morning, baln delivers a Context Card that explains market movements in four layers:

1. Historical Context
"This pattern happened in 2008 too -- and recovered within 6 months."

2. Macro Chain
"US CPI release -> Rate hike fears -> Tech selloff -> Your Samsung shares followed."

3. Institutional Behavior
"Foreign investors sold for 3 days straight -- but it's rebalancing season, not panic."

4. Your Portfolio Impact
"Your portfolio dropped 1.2%, but your Health Score is unchanged."

Result: "Oh, today's dip wasn't my fault -- it's macro." That's the calm we deliver.
